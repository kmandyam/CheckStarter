CheckStarter - By Karishma Mandyam

CheckStarter is a tool for anyone who seeks to simplify day to day tasks. This app was inspired by Atul Gawande and his research on how checklists significantly decrease mortality rates in hostpitals by holding doctors accountable for every step of each procedure. Gawande also mentions that checklists are a basic and useful tool for anyone who performs complex procedures on a daily basis, including pilots, technicians, and construction workers. For more information, check out The Checklist Manifesto by Atul Gawande
